Diabetes Care & Management

Evidence-based diabetes treatment with personalised glycaemic control — backed by postgraduate training at Boston University, USA.

Boston University Certified 16+ Years Experience Arete Hospitals OPD

Overview

Diabetes is a chronic metabolic condition affecting how your body processes blood glucose. Left unmanaged, it can lead to serious complications affecting the heart, kidneys, eyes, and nerves. Early diagnosis and consistent specialist care are essential for maintaining quality of life.

At Arete Hospitals, Dr. Sandeep Reddy offers a root-cause approach — combining medication optimisation, dietary guidance, and regular monitoring to help patients achieve and maintain target HbA1c levels.

Conditions We Treat

  • Type 1 Diabetes Mellitus
  • Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus
  • Gestational Diabetes
  • Pre-Diabetes & Insulin Resistance
  • Diabetic Ketoacidosis (DKA) Recovery
  • Diabetic Complications Screening

Our Treatment Approach

  1. Comprehensive Assessment

    Full metabolic workup including HbA1c, fasting glucose, lipid profile, kidney function, and foot examination.

  2. Personalised Treatment Plan

    Tailored medication regimen — oral agents, GLP-1 agonists, or insulin therapy based on your profile and goals.

  3. Lifestyle Integration

    Diet counselling, activity planning, and weight management strategies aligned with your daily routine.

  4. Ongoing Monitoring

    Regular follow-ups to adjust therapy, screen for complications, and keep you on track for long-term health.
